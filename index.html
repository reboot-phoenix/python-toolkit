Design decisions

  • secrets over random — uses the OS CSPRNG, not a pseudo-random generator. Matters for anything security-related.
  • PBKDF2 at 480k iterations — follows OWASP 2023 recommendation for PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256, making brute-force infeasible.
  • Fernet for symmetric encryption — authenticated AES. Tampered ciphertext raises an exception rather than silently decrypting garbage.
  • Salt stored separately — never embedded in the vault file, so the vault alone is useless without the salt file.
